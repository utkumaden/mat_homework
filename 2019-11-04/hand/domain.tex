\subsection{Domain}

The domain of the function can be found simply by inspecting it's components.

\[
\begin{aligned}
    x^2 & : \mathbb{R} \rightarrow \mathbb{R} \\
    e^x & : \mathbb{R} \rightarrow \mathbb{R} \\
    \text{Therefore } \\
    x^2\cdot e^x & : \mathbb{R} \rightarrow \mathbb{R}
\end{aligned}
\]
